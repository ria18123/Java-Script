Student Life

Park

Woodland University has always been a firm believer in being surrounded by nature. It contains a large park where students may exercise their independence. Students are surrounded by nature, which provides a wonderful calming and de-stressing environment.

Student Canteen

Woodland University has a spacious Canteen for students so that students may interact more freely. We believe that a focused and healthy mind begins with a good diet and a healthy person, thus the cafeteria provides a variety of products, the majority of which are health conscious.

Student Library

Woodland University's massive Library houses a diverse collection of literature. In a major institution, the library provides a vast room with a quiet and light environment so that students may concentrate on their studies.

Student Cafeteria

While studying or rushing to class or the library, students can stop at Woodland University's wonderful and quiet outdoor cafe for a variety of beverages such as coffee, smoothies, and other munchies.

Conference Room & Halls

Woodland University provides open and spacious conference rooms and halls to accommodate the high number of students so that everyone may study comfortably. Its classrooms include cutting-edge technology and are all digitally equipped.

Sports Ground

Woodland University offers and has sponsored a variety of sports that student-athletes participate in, and the campus is equipped to facilitate varied activities and sports practice.

Extracurricular Activities

Lacrosse

Woodland University student-athletes compete in college lacrosse. Men's and women's field lacrosse are played on the varsity and club levels at Woodland University.

Basketball

Basketball is a sport played in groups. Because it is one of the most popular sports, it has become an important part of university traditions. Woodland University student-athletes participate in college basketball because we feel it will aid in team building and retaining the university's spirit.

football

In the United States, American football is the most popular sport. It is a team sport that has long been utilized to promote teamwork, principles, interests, and national pride at Woodland University.

Music & Instruments

WU hosts a variety of music & instrument events to showcase our students' skills. We have music & instrument groups, & we urge students to join and engage in them in order to make use of the resources available.

Outdoor Excursion & trips

WU has upheld its practice of taking students on academic excursions & trips since, we believe that seeing and experiencing more is the greatest way to learn more.

Donation & Charity campaigns

Woodland University supports and has supported a variety of social groups and initiatives, and we encourage our students to volunteer or donate to these organizations.

About Us

Welcome to Woodland Community College, one of California’s 2015 top-ranked community colleges!

Our objectives remain the same: to provide high-quality student services and academic programs that enable achieving your educational and career goals simpler. To that end, we want to make sure that students have access to the right student support services for a smooth matriculation process and life-long learning techniques that will serve as the basis for future career and academic exploration. Many transfer degrees and certificate programs are available at Woodland Community College. Whether you want to obtain an associate's degree and transfer to a four-year university or get a specialized certificate, Woodland Community College offers a program that will meet your needs.
